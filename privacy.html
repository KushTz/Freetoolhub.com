✅ Short version: FreeToolHub.com does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All tools run entirely in your browser. We do not track you.

1. Who We Are

FreeToolHub.com is a free online tools website that provides utilities including a Password Generator, Word Counter, Age Calculator, and QR Code Generator. Our website is located at https://freetoolhub.com.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be very clear about what we do not collect:

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or any personal information
  • We do NOT store passwords you generate on our site
  • We do NOT store text you enter in our Word Counter
  • We do NOT store your date of birth from the Age Calculator
  • We do NOT store URLs or text you enter in our QR Code Generator
  • We do NOT require you to create an account or log in

All tools on FreeToolHub.com run entirely within your browser. No data is sent to our servers.

3. Cookies

FreeToolHub.com itself does not use cookies. However, third-party services we use (such as Google AdSense) may use cookies to serve relevant advertisements. These cookies are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google's Ad Settings at adssettings.google.com.

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit.

6. Children's Privacy

FreeToolHub.com does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our tools are designed for general use and do not target children specifically.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
